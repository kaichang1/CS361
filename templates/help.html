Frequently Asked Questions

To start analyzing MarketWatch articles, simply click on Analyze in the top navigation bar. From there, you can either click a recent article on the left, or enter an article URL on the right. If you are entering a custom URL, make sure to click Go afterwards.

Make sure you have properly entered the entire URL of a MarketWatch article. We currently does not support other article types.

We provide detailed explanations of all analyses in our results. Give it a try!

We are currently working on two big updates! In the first update, we plan to add a summarizer to summarize the article. In the second update, we plan to implement support for aggregate analysis of multiple articles at once.
